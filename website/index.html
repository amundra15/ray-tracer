Concept

While brainstorming for the scene, we agreed that we want to keep the scene elements simple and want to focus on the details to showcase the features of our ray tracer, and generate an image which looks realistic. Going through rendering competitions of some institutes, the Utah teapot (the quintessential Graphics object) really resonated with us. Wanting to incorporate that, we designed our scene around it. The result is our rendition (and rendering) of what a weekend morning looks like.
